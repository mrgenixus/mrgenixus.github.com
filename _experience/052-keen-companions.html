Keen Companions is an elder-assistive technology platform focused on improving quality of life for seniors who are increasingly isolated in their homes or care centers, away from family and society. It provides interactive companionship through an animated character delivered on a dedicated tablet device, monitoring mood, activity, and user interaction, while generating summarized insights from conversations for caregivers and family members. The system also guides seniors in capturing personal stories and anecdotes, building a long-term digital memory book for their families. As Engineering Lead, I own full-stack development and infrastructure across backend systems, frontend applications, and native Android, as well as AWS infrastructure, deployment processes, and version control strategy, supporting scalable, AI-driven user experiences that improve engagement and caregiver visibility.
